Our Story

Born from a love of light

Whispering Flames started as a creative project in Itahari-4, Sunsari and grew into a passion-driven handmade candle business. Every candle is poured with care using natural wax, cotton wicks, and premium fragrance oils.

What truly sets us apart is our signature specialty — we hide your personal message inside the candle, waiting to be revealed by the flame. A gift that speaks without words.
